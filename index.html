VARIOLITE

 

Variations Augment Real Iterative Outcomes Letting Information Transcend Exploration

Variolite is also a kind of rock formation. Image credit Schaferpho

Variolite is a package for Atom that allows programmers to locally version their code. This system will appear in CHI 2017.

Mary Beth Kery, Amber Horvath and Brad A. Myers.  “Variolite: Supporting Exploratory Programming by Data Scientists,” Proceedings CHI'2017: Human Factors in Computing Systems, Denver, CO,  May 6-11, 2017. To appear. [preprint pdf]
